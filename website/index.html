what is avime???

Avime is the first fully on-chain customizable PFP NFT project (Launched Sept 3, 2021), featuring artwork heavily inspired by anime. Our custom built contract, on-chain provenance, and customization mechanics make Avime truly stand out in the world of NFTs. With a plethora of customization options, the ability to choose between two body types, Avime gives you the ability to create an avatar that you truly resonate with.
Just collect the traits you like and fuse them!


each avime mint will give you 6 different avime trait cards of varying rarities, one from each of these categories:


Avime Trait Cards are ERC721 NFTs that can be traded on secondary markets, and can be combined in the Wardrobe to create a completed Avime. All Trait Cards combined will be burned upon fusion, meaning the total circulating supply of traits will reduce over time.


you will need one trait for each category in order to create an avime


There will only ever be enough Trait Cards to create 10k total Avime. Future expansion packs are possible to add more options to customization, but if expansion packs are released, an exchange of already existing Avime Trait Cards will be required.

frequently asked questions
What does it mean to be on-chain?

Having Avime entirely encoded on-chain means that this minting website, the meta-data and the images used to create your Avime and their traits live directly on the Ethereum blockchain. This means that there is no requirement for Avime assets to be hosted on services such as IPFS or centralized servers. This means that your Avime is an immortal and native resident of Ethereum that will be backed by the robust security the network can provide.

How are Avime customizable?

We created Avime with the intent for everyone to make characters that truly resonate with them. Avime allows you to mix and match your character the way that you want. There are two smart contracts: an initial minting contract and a fusion contract. When minting, you’ll receive 6 individual Avime Trait Cards required for creating an Avime; a background, body, face, clothes, hair, and accessory. In the Wardrobe, you can preview how traits look with one another and choose whether you want your Avime to be male or female. Each trait is its own ERC721 NFT that can be bought, sold, or traded on the open market. When you’ve collected the traits you want to use, you can fuse them together to mint your Avime! All Trait Cards are burned in the process, and there will only ever be enough traits to create 10k Avime, so choose wisely and make sure your combination is the one you really want. All assets including your finished Avime are stored directly on-chain!

Will I be able to choose what traits I receive?

Upon minting, you will be given 6 randomly generated Avime Trait Cards from each category. Trait Cards have a rarity system which dictates your probability to receive them upon mint. However, you are not required to combine your traits into an Avime upon mint, they will instead be minted into your wallet individually where you can even mix and match with previously minted cards. This means that while you cannot choose the traits you will receive on mint, you will have the flexibility to track down specific traits you want on the open market to create your Avime!

Are you going to add more traits?

The contracts were designed to be extendable to allow for more traits. We would love to add traits, but it's really up to the community. If we do add more traits, all Avime Trait Cards from expansions will be compatible with Season 1 Trait cards in the Wardrobe, significantly increasing the amount of available customization options! In order to maintain a maximum cap of 10k total Avime Fusions, an exchange of Season 1 Trait Cards will be required for minting those from Season 2 within the same category. Like Season 1 Trait Cards, there would be varying degrees of rarity in the expansion set. The rarity of the Season 1 Trait Card burned during the exchange will bear no effect on the Season 2 Trait Card received. This means that Common Avime Trait Cards from Season 1 have a chance to re-roll into rarer Season 2 traits!

Can you create an avime trait of my favorite anime character?

We respect the intellectual properties of other creators and believe the future of Avime would be jeopardized should we add traits that directly copy from already existing works. While we will not create direct copies of character traits, we take inspiration from a wide variety of anime, video games, and other media to create original designs that capture the aesthetic of various popular characters and tropes.

Can someone create the same Avime combination as mine?

Due to the ability to choose your own traits, it is possible for someone to copy combinations from others. However, if the Wardrobe detects that the exact combination of traits being used is not unique, you will get a notification prior to fusion. You will not be prevented from creating this fusion however, as it is entirely up to the user’s choice. Note that Avime are serialized in order of when they were created. This means that the first uniquely created Avime combination will always have a lower serialization number than any copies that come after it.

How much does it cost to mint and fuse?

It costs 0.09 ETH for a pack of 6 Avime Trait Cards, and each transaction is limited to 10 mints maximum. Using the Wardrobe to create an Avime Fusion will cost .01 ETH.
