Derp Vault

The Derp Vault is an experiment that uses 80% of mint funds to establish an automatic market maker and loan provider inside of the Derp Nation ERC721 smart contract.

The vault enforces a spread between buy and sell prices at all times.

After the vault collateralizes all 20k NFTs at 50% of mint price, price discovery begins

For more information check out the contract on etherscan

Note: If you have issues with this site, send me a tweet and try the original vault

About Me

I like to experiment on the ETH blockchain. If you like what I'm doing, you can help support me by minting a DerpNation PFP and becoming a member of DerpNation.

Note: I have started the slow process of decentralized all my websites to derp://projectName.og, but it will take a while. The links below go to the normal internet. I will add info here as I update projects


LNR-Web - Feb 2023

LNR-Ethers - Jan 2023
Created a javascript library to easily interact with the Linagee ecosystem. LNR-ethers


cSVG - Jan 2023
Created a smart contract and javascript library to compress/decompress and render SVGs in order to save >60% gas when storing SVGs in chain. (Eth state not calldata) Currently this is live on Sepolia and will be going on mainnet after some community testing. Check it out


.PFP - Dec 2022
Created a smart contract to turn any NFT into an ENS-like domain. If you own BAYC #123, you can receive ETH and NFTs by having people send them to 123.bayc.pfp. You can also create a profile where you attach any information you want to your NFT, or issue soul bound tokens to other NFTs. Check it out


LNR RESOLVER - Nov 2022
Created a resolver for LNR names allowing for funds and NFTs to be sent to "name.og"


LNR IDENTITY - Oct 2022
Created a prototype identity contract allowing for arbitrary user data to be attached to a LNR name


LNR SWAP - Oct 2022
Created a trading contract and basic UI to give degens a place to trade unwrapped LNR names. The first on-chain trading for the oldest NFT contract on ETH happened here.
